Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Mentari

What is Mentari and how does it help with mental wellness?

Mentari is a free mental wellness app that provides personalized daily challenges based on your goals and preferences. You'll receive tailored challenges with guided resources to help you build better habits, reduce stress, and improve mental clarity. Share your progress with friends and track your journey to better mental health.

Is Mentari free to use?

Yes! Mentari is free to download and use with no ads. We believe mental wellness should be accessible to everyone, so we're keeping the app free as we grow and gather feedback from our community.

How do daily challenges work in Mentari?

After setting up your profile with your goals and preferences, Mentari provides personalized daily challenges tailored specifically to you. Each challenge includes guidance to help you complete it and resources to dive deeper. These can include mindfulness exercises, physical activities, journaling prompts, or social connection tasks. Complete them, share with friends, and track your progress over time.

What platforms is Mentari available on?

Mentari is available on both iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android devices. You can download it from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

How does Mentari compare to other mental health apps?

Unlike many mental health apps that focus solely on meditation, Mentari offers diverse daily challenges, community support, and no ads. We focus on small, actionable steps that create lasting change, making mental wellness achievable for everyone.

Can I use Mentari if I've never done mindfulness or wellness practices before?

Absolutely! Mentari is designed for beginners and experienced practitioners alike. Our challenges are simple, bite-sized, and come with guidance to help you understand why each activity matters and how to get the most from it.

Do I need to complete challenges every day?

Not at all! Mentari is designed to be flexible and fit your lifestyle. While building a daily habit can be beneficial, you can use the app at your own pace. There's no pressure - just support when you need it.

How long does each challenge take?

Most challenges take between 2-15 minutes to complete. We believe in making mental wellness accessible through small, manageable tasks that fit into your busy day.
